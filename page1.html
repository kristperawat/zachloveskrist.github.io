"destruction of cognitive bias through the internet."

The internet is a catalyst not only to the spread information around the world, but also to the sentiment that the online world now has a much bigger influence on human behavior than before. The so called 'cognitive bias', which is the deviation from rationality of judgement from a human's "subjective reality," may very well be the cause of the rapid modernization of society and it's shift towards a more online-based perspective. Outside influences like social media content virtually creates a high possibility of affecting a person's values and morals, most of which are clouding one’s judgement with both positive and negative outcomes.

Free speech exists and manifests itself in the internet. With the current age of rapid technological globalization and advancement, majority of people have access to the world wide web; in turn having more access to a plethora of different beliefs and opinions expressed by countless people around the world. Theoretically, however, things that circulate around these cyberspaces include acts of hate, bigotry, and other negative ideas; all of which having direct cynical and obstructive impact to many people. These, combined with the sheer increase of internet users over the past few years explain the worryingly negative direction human subjectivity is headed because of the growing influential power the net possesses.

Destruction of a major component of present humans’ lives on a global scale not just a simple walk in the park. Many factors, including budget restrictions and potential backlash all pose a threat to the success of removing all internet-caused cognitive bias from the face of this planet. This immediately rules out the possibility of demolishing all communication services and companies; as it would leave a deep scar in the global economy. Despite this, one theoretically easier and more effective way of shutting down the internet is to legalize and encourage government-controlled distributed-denial-of-service (DDoS) attacks on all major servers, inciting website attacks all over the world; which can effectively and potentially destroy the internet.
